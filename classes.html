Classes and Spa


There are different sessions available which are strictly segregated, childrens classes, family and mixed sessions. For every sessions, there are 2 to 3 instructors providing support at all times.


We also offer group bookings, one-to-one bookings as well as block booking classes. One to one classes are for a duration of 60 minutes whilst group bookings are 90 minutes. Our Instructors during classes or swimming sessions are attentive and supportive and well experienced. We offer a 30 minutes free trial class. The Spa is led by trained aestheticians and massage therapists to help you relax and enjoy your time.


To book please call us directly or send an email to emeraldpool@hc.spa.co.uk.
